FAQ

Confident prep starts with clarity.

Answers to the most frequent questions from Sri Lankan O/L candidates.

How should I prioritise subjects?

Schedule heavier blocks for subjects you find hardest while keeping daily touchpoints for confident ones. The planner suggestions keep this balance.

How often should I take mock exams?

Integrate a full mock every two weeks once you enter the final 90 days. Use the resources tab to pull past papers.

What is a healthy daily workload?

Between 4–6 focused hours with clear breaks is sustainable for most students. The hour logger helps you stay within that range.

Can I revise effectively with limited time?

Yes. Focus on active recall, timed drills, and sprint sessions. The live tools page keeps these prompts handy.

How do I keep motivation high?

Track small wins, log hours visibly, and rotate subjects to avoid burnout. Use the mindset vault for quick reminders.
